# Project Brief

## Product

WorkMate is an Ireland-first services marketplace where customers post jobs and verified providers submit offers.

## Primary Users

- Customers in Ireland posting service requests.
- Providers (pros) applying, verifying identity/documents, and quoting jobs.
- Admins reviewing jobs, provider applications, and documents.

## Product Goals

- Trusted local job matching with strong verification signals.
- Controlled payments using Stripe Connect secure hold and capture.
- Compliance-ready operations for Ireland market and EU context.

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- English-only UI, docs, errors, and policies.
- Ireland-first jurisdiction decisions for onboarding, KYC, and disputes.
- Strict RLS posture in Supabase schema and APIs.
