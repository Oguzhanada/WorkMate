# WorkMate User Guide

WorkMate is an Ireland-first services marketplace that connects customers with verified local service providers. This guide covers everything you need to get started — whether you are hiring or offering services.

---

## For Customers

### 1. Creating an Account

1. Visit [workmate.ie](https://workmate.ie) and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your email address and choose a password, or sign in with Google.
3. Confirm your email address via the link sent to your inbox.
4. Your account is created with the **customer** role by default.

---

### 2. Posting a Job

From your dashboard, click **Post a Job** and complete the multi-step form:

- **Category** — Select the service type (e.g. Plumbing, Cleaning, Childcare).
- **Description** — Describe the work in detail. Use the **AI-write** button to generate a professional description automatically.
- **Location** — Enter your full address including a valid **Eircode** (e.g. `D02 XY45`). This is required so providers can confirm they cover your area.
- **Budget** — Indicate your expected budget range. This helps providers submit relevant quotes.
- **Availability** — Choose preferred dates and times.

Once submitted, verified providers in your area will receive a job alert and can submit quotes.

---

### 3. Reviewing Quotes and Offers

Quotes appear on your job page ranked by a composite score that weighs:

- **Price** — lower quotes rank higher, all else equal.
- **Provider rating** — average score across quality, communication, punctuality, and value.
- **Compliance** — verified providers (SafePass, insurance, tax clearance, Garda vetting where applicable) are ranked higher.
- **Response time** — providers who respond quickly are boosted.

Click any quote to view the provider's full profile, reviews, and credentials before deciding.

---

### 4. Accepting a Quote and Secure Payment Hold

1. Click **Accept Quote** on the offer you want.
2. You will be taken to a Stripe-powered payment screen.
3. Your payment is placed on a **secure hold** — it is not released to the provider yet. Your card is charged, but the funds are held in escrow until the job is complete.
4. The provider is notified and the job moves to **In Progress** status.

---

### 5. Completing a Job and Releasing Payment

Once the work is finished to your satisfaction:

1. Go to the job page and click **Mark as Complete**.
2. Confirm the job is done — this releases the held payment to the provider.

If you are not satisfied, do not mark the job complete. Use the dispute process instead (see below).

---

### 6. Leaving a Review

After a job is marked complete you will be prompted to leave a review. Ratings are across four dimensions:

| Dimension | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Quality** | Standard of the work delivered |
| **Communication** | Responsiveness and clarity throughout the job |
| **Punctuality** | Arrived and completed on time |
| **Value** | Worth the price paid |

Each dimension is rated 1–5 stars. Your review is published publicly on the provider's profile and feeds into their composite ranking score.

---

### 7. Rebooking a Provider

If you rebook a provider you have previously worked with, WorkMate applies a **reduced platform fee of 1.9%** (versus the standard rate). The discount is applied automatically when you accept a new quote from the same provider.

---

### 8. Saving Favourite Providers

On any provider's profile page, click the **Save** (heart) button to add them to your Saved Providers list.

Access your saved providers from the **Saved** link in the navigation menu, or from the **Saved Providers** widget on your dashboard. This makes it easy to rebook trusted tradespeople quickly.

---

## For Providers

### 1. Creating a Provider Account

1. Sign up at [workmate.ie](https://workmate.ie) as normal.
2. From your dashboard, click **Become a Provider** and complete the provider onboarding flow.
3. Your account gains the **verified_pro** role once your credentials are reviewed and approved.

---

### 2. Completing Your Profile

A complete profile gets more visibility in search results and quote rankings.

- **Services** — Add all service categories you offer.
- **Availability** — Set your typical working hours and mark days you are available for same-day bookings (earns a **Same-Day Available** badge).
- **Location** — Set your base location and the radius you cover.
- **About** — Write a short bio and highlight your experience.
- **Portfolio** — Upload photos of completed work.

---

### 3. Verification Requirements

WorkMate has two verification layers. Completing both increases your ranking and unlocks more jobs.

**Identity verification** (via Stripe Identity):
- Government-issued photo ID

**Business/compliance verification** (documents reviewed by WorkMate admin):

| Document | Who needs it |
|---|---|
| SafePass card | Construction-related services |
| Public Liability Insurance | All providers (recommended) |
| Tax Clearance Certificate | All providers operating commercially |

Upload documents from your profile under **Verification**. The WorkMate team reviews submissions within 1–2 business days.

---

### 4. Garda Vetting

Garda vetting is **required** for any provider offering services in the following categories:

- Childcare and babysitting
- Eldercare and home care
- Cleaning or maintenance with unsupervised home access

To request Garda vetting:

1. Go to your profile under **Compliance**.
2. Submit a Garda vetting request — WorkMate will provide instructions and a reference number.
3. Once cleared, a **Garda Vetted** badge appears on your profile.

Garda vetting clearance has an expiry date. You will be notified before it lapses so you can renew.

---

### 5. Responding to Job Alerts

When a customer posts a job that matches your services and location, you will receive a **job alert** by email and in-app notification.

Click the alert to view the full job details. You can then submit a quote directly from the job page.

---

### 6. Submitting Quotes and Offers

On a job you are eligible for:

1. Click **Submit Quote**.
2. Enter your proposed price (in EUR).
3. Add a message explaining your approach, timeline, and relevant experience.
4. Submit — the customer can see your quote alongside others.

You can edit or withdraw a quote at any time before it is accepted.

---

### 7. Subscription Tiers

WorkMate offers three subscription tiers for providers:

| Tier | Price | What's included |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | Free | Limited quote submissions per month, basic profile |
| **Professional** | €29/month | Unlimited quotes, priority ranking boost, verified badge prominence |
| **Premium** | €59/month | Everything in Professional, plus featured placement, direct booking link, and advanced analytics |

Manage your subscription from the **Subscription** section of your dashboard.

---

### 8. Getting Paid

WorkMate uses **Stripe Connect** to handle all payments securely.

1. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding from your provider dashboard — this links your bank account for payouts.
2. When a customer accepts your quote, their payment is placed on hold.
3. Once the customer marks the job as complete, the funds are released to your Stripe Connect account.
4. Payouts typically arrive in your bank account within 2–5 business days, depending on your bank.

WorkMate deducts a platform fee from the released amount. The fee is reduced to **1.9%** for repeat bookings with the same customer.

## General

### Dispute Resolution

If a job does not go as expected:

1. **Do not mark the job as complete** if the work is unsatisfactory.
2. Use the **Raise a Dispute** option on the job page.
3. Describe the issue — both parties can submit evidence (photos, messages).
4. The WorkMate team reviews the dispute and makes a decision within 5 business days.
5. Outcomes: full release to provider, full refund to customer, or a negotiated partial payment.

WorkMate's decision on disputes is final. For formal legal recourse, Irish consumer law applies.
